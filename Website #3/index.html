My Hero: Red, the M&M

"Everything is under control."

  • Someone who I consider to be an inspiration to many, but loved by all is Red.
  • Red is a talkative personality who is sarcastic and cranky.
  • He is the spokescandy for M&Ms
  • The main M&M in nearly every M&M commercial and everyone's favorite colored M&M! Who can resist him?
Most Frequently Asked Questions
  • Q: How did Red become such an inspiration to you?
